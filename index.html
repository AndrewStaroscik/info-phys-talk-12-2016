“These physical models complement displays in digital format by providing a hard-copy frame of reference that can be touched and manipulated with optimum hand-eye coordination and immediate results.” - Acevedo et al, 2004
We engage with information differently in space. There is something more 'real' about an object you can pick up, feel the texture of or even just see in real space.

Benefits

  • Provide an engaging experience
  • Offer novel perspective
  • Increase memorability
  • Allow personal connection to information
  • Improve understanding of scales and relationships
  • Make an emotional Impact

Technical Considerations

  • Process does not scale
    • Numbers
    • Size
  • 2d layering introduces mechanical weaknesses
  • Limited number of geometries possible
  • Technical skill and proper equipment required
